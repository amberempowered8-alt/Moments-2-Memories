Our Story

Two Friends, One Dream

We are Bre and Amber, two friends based in the heart of the Verde Valley who believe that love deserves to be celebrated with intention, beauty, and care. What started as a shared passion for creating magical moments has grown into something we couldn't be more proud of.

Moments 2 Memories was born from the simple belief that every love story deserves its perfect scene. Whether it's a candlelit proposal beneath Sedona's red rocks, an intimate anniversary surprise, or a birthday celebration that takes your breath away — we design experiences that you'll carry with you forever.

From the first balloon to the last rose petal, every detail is placed with love.
