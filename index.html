Moments 2 Memories — Luxury Experience Agreement

1. DESIGN LOCK & BOOKING POLICIES
Section 1.1: The 72-Hour Design Lock. To ensure we can source and prepare your custom decor, all design choices, color schemes, custom signs, and venue access details must be finalized and signed off within 72 hours of your initial booking. If these details are not finalized within this window, your date may be released back into our booking calendar to accommodate other clients.

Section 1.2: Venue & Lodging Coordination. Moments 2 Memories provides styling and decoration services only. The client is completely responsible for booking the primary space (such as a hotel room, private rental, or permitted outdoor location). Our team cannot begin preparing or loading inventory until you provide verified proof of your room reservation, check-in times, and explicit permission from the property for our team to enter and decorate.

2. PAYMENT POLICIES & DECOR MANAGEMENT
Section 2.1: Pre-Authorization & Hold Deposit. To officially submit your date request, a valid payment method must be provided. The foundational experience fee acts as a pre-authorization hold to protect your calendar slot. No funds will be withdrawn or charged until our design team has personally reviewed your submission, verified availability, and formally confirmed your date and event details.

3. ACCESSIBILITY, LIABILITY, & COMMUNICATION BOUNDARY
Section 3.1: Professional Communication Standards. Moments 2 Memories operates as a premium design office. To ensure every detail of your setup is perfectly tracked and executed without mistakes, we rely on structured written blueprints and scheduled consultation calls. We kindly request that clients use our formal messaging channels rather than continuous, unscheduled text messages or phone calls.
